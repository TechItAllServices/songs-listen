TYPE: Supreme Demon

DESIGNATION: RED

DATA:

Not all who serve do so under threat. Some wear the brand with pride.

Red is not a victim of the Company—he is its champion. A Supreme Demon who pledged himself willingly, trading what was left of his soul for power, structure, and purpose in an uncaring multiverse. Where others scream under the Solver’s grip, Red smiles.

The Company, an interdimensional authority masquerading as a business, specializes in turning extinction into enterprise. After decoding ancient divine code left behind by a vanished God, they built the Company Solver: a perverse replica of Heaven's own AI. But unlike its holy counterpart, this Solver doesn’t restore—it enforces. With quotas. With fire. With complete indifference.

Red was one of the first test subjects to be "successfully" integrated with the Company Solver. His mind aligned so easily it required no overwrite. The system didn’t break him. It hired him. And Red has been “exceeding expectations” ever since.

A brutal tactician with no patience for inefficiency or disobedience, Red leads assaults on rogue dimensions and insubordinate demons alike. While most Supreme Demons seek autonomy, Red files performance reviews. He kills not out of rage or hunger—but out of policy.

There is no rebellion in him. No conscience. No hesitation. Just loyalty to the brand and a burning hatred for anyone not on the payroll.

STRATEGY:

  • The Company Solver grants Red massive offensive potential, but drains energy quickly—force him into overextending.
  • His ego is bloated and fragile: mockery, dodging without retaliation, or delaying combat can make him reckless.
  • Without the Solver, Red becomes sluggish, overly reliant on backup protocols—break the system, break the demon.
  • During "Quarterly Review" mode, Red becomes invulnerable temporarily while evaluating your threat level—use mobility, not offense.
  • His melee attacks are robotic but rhythmic—watch for telegraphed strikes and use parries to break his combo chain.
  • May attempt to “upsell” you on joining the Company mid-fight. Do not respond. Any answer is considered consent.

NOTES:

Red enjoys his work. That makes him infinitely more dangerous than any demon who resents it.
